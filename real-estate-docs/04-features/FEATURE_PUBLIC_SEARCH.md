# FEATURE_PUBLIC_SEARCH

## Purpose
Let visitors search approved real estate listings with practical MVP filters.

## Public Routes
- `/`
- `/properties`
- `/properties/[slug]`

## Filters
- keyword
- listing purpose
- property type
- division
- district
- city
- area
- min price
- max price
- bedrooms
- bathrooms
- min size
- max size
- land size
- price visibility

## Sort
- newest
- price low to high
- price high to low
- size large to small
- featured first

## Public Privacy Rule
Search/detail responses must never include owner phone, owner email, private address, or admin notes.

## SEO Rule
Approved listing detail pages are indexable. Dashboard/admin/auth pages are noindex.
